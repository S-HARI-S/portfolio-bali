King Mahabali is considered to be the noblest and most prosperous ruler, who transformed his kingdom into a heavenly place.

Mahabali is considered one of the Chiranjivi, a group of seven immortals. It is believed that he will become the King of Swarga (Heaven) in the next yuga.

Mahabali also temporarily possessed the amrita (nectar for eternal life) obtained by the asuras. The amrita allowed his subjects to bring him back to life after his death in one of the wars between suras (devas) and asuras.Mahabali was, thus, immune from death. After many wars, the invincible Bali had conquered heaven and earth.
